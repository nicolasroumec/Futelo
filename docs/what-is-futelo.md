# What is Futelo? ⚽

[Español](./que-es-futelo.md) · 🌐 English

Futelo is an app to keep track of your football video game tournaments with
friends (FIFA, eFootball, whatever you play). Each group gets its own space with
seasons, standings, stats and history.

It's built for groups that play **1 versus 1**: every match is one player against
another, and the app takes care of building the fixture, computing the standings
and keeping everyone's all-time ranking.

---

## The idea in one sentence

Create a group, invite your friends, and each season you can play a **League**, a
**Cup** and a **SuperCup**. The app sets everything up — you just enter the results.

---

## How is it organized?

- **Group (Vault):** your crew. One person owns and manages it.
- **Season:** lives inside a group. You can have several over time. Each one starts
  as a draft, is activated when it's ready, and is closed when it's over.
- **Competitions** inside each season (you pick which ones):
  - **League** — everyone plays everyone.
  - **Cup** — single elimination, with brackets.
  - **SuperCup** — the final between the League champion and the Cup champion.

---

## What can you do today?

### Set up the group and add people
- Create your group and edit the player list.
- Invite friends **by email** or **by link** to join.

### Play a League
- The app builds the **fixture** automatically (everyone plays everyone).
- You can **reshuffle** it before the first match is played.
- Optional **home-and-away** mode.
- Enter each result and the table updates on its own.
- Configurable **tiebreaker** rules (goal difference, head-to-head, or a combination).
- Matchday view, with a pending-matches indicator per player.
- Edit a match (team, video game, date) or **correct the last result** you entered
  if you made a mistake.

### Play a Cup
- The **bracket** is generated automatically.
- You choose how the ties are seeded: by season ranking, by league position, or at
  random.
- **Home-and-away** with an optional away-goal rule.
- Supports decision **on penalties**.
- Visual bracket view with each tie's aggregate score.

### Play a SuperCup
- Single match (or home-and-away) between the League and Cup champions.
- Shows the head-to-head history between the two.
- Supports penalties.

### View stats
- **General ranking** (everyone's all-time ELO) and **season ranking**.
- **Honours:** titles won by each player.
- **Top scorers.**
- **ELO history** of each player in a chart.
- **Head-to-head** between two players.
- **Group records** (streaks, etc.).
- Ranking by video game and a per-team panel.
- Player profile with recent form.

### Manual entry
- If you already played the matches elsewhere, you can **enter results by hand** in
  any of the three competitions, without following the generated fixture.

### Conveniences
- **Light / dark mode.**
- **Spanish and English.**
- It's a **PWA**: installs on your phone like an app and works offline.
- A **season recap** page you can share by link.

---

## How is it scored?

Every result you enter updates both players' **ELO** — the same rating system chess
uses. Beating someone ranked above you is worth more than beating someone below.
There's an all-time ELO (you carry it forever) and a per-season one (starts from
zero each time).

---

## In one line

Futelo turns casual matches between friends into a real tournament: with a table,
a cup, stats and an undisputed champion at the end of every season.
